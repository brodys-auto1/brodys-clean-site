Our Story

A Dealership Born from a Better Idea

Brody's Auto was founded with one goal in mind: give people in our community a dealership they could actually trust. Too many buyers walk away from car lots feeling pressured, confused, or shortchanged — we set out to change that.

What started as a small lot with a handful of vehicles has grown into one of the area's most respected used car dealerships. We specialize in quality used cars, utility trucks, and Sprinter vans — every single one hand-picked and put through a thorough inspection before it ever hits our lot.

We're proud to be locally owned and operated. When you do business with us, you're supporting a family business that genuinely cares about its neighbors.
